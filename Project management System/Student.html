Important Guidelines to be followed by the Students

  • The Project activity is scheduled to begin on May 1st, 2023 and will continue for a total of 12 weeks, ending on July 24th, 2023.
  • Attendance at weekly and monthly reviews is mandatory, and we expect all participants to attend these meetings
  • The weekly reviews will be held every Monday at 10am, and the monthly reviews will be held on the first Friday of every month at 2pm.
  • We also require students to submit weekly documentation and a monthly report to the guide.
  • The deadline for submitting weekly documentation will be every Friday by 5pm, starting from May 5th, 2023. The deadline for submitting the monthly report will be on the last Friday of every month by 5pm, starting from May 26th, 2023.
